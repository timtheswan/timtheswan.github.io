GameJam

By Tim Swanson on May 21, 2019

The TU CSE GameJam is a game jam series hosted every semester by the CSE Department and Dr. Jon Denning and run by students at Taylor University. A game jam is a short, fun, and intense party during which students get together to develop a video game from scratch. Regardless of major or knowledge of programming, all TU students are invited to participate!

Systems Option

on May 21, 2019

What is Systems? Systems is not a minor in Computer Science. The goal of the Systems curriculum is to help students recognize that the world is filled with systems (like educational, distribution, and manufacturing systems). It is the processes inhabiting these systems that produce value and help organizations achieve high performance. Our graduates are enjoying careers at enterprises of all sizes, including global consulting companies.   What in the world is Accenture selling?

Abet Accreditation

on May 20, 2019

Our Engineering & Computer Engineering programs are ABET-accredited. Accreditation The Engineering & Computer Engineering degree programs are accredited by the Engineering Accreditation Commission of ABET (https://www.abet.org). Program Educational Objectives Prepare our graduates to server others dependably, most importantly their employer, customers, and community. Prepare our graduates to practice technical competence, producing reliable engineering designs. Prepare our graduates to exercise creativity in their work, fostering innovative solutions.
