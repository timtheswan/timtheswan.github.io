Faculty and Staff

The Computer Science and Engineering faculty and staff are a group of highly qualified professionals with expertise and experiences that include software engineering, artificial intelligence, leadership in major corporations, ethics in technology, and computing standards in tax codes. They each value interaction with students both inside and outside of the classroom.

The faculty and staff care about the students as mentors and friends. Their conversations can range anywhere from the classic Star Wars/Star Trek/Avengers talk to deep theology or philosophy. Through their combined experiences and skills, our faculty and staff are able to glorify God and give everything to Him.

About us

Highly acclaimed for our simultaneous academic rigor and flourishing community, we equip students with the skills and environment they need to reach their full potential. We believe that students grow best in a Christ-centered community, which is why our dedicated Christian faculty members take a personal interest in our student’s growth and education as individuals.
