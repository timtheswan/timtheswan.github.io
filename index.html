Services

Photographer & Videographer

My field of study includes a number of classes in video and audio production as well as photography. Much of the photography in my websites and graphic design are mine. Take a look at my Instagram Photography account, and portfolio.

Website Design and Development

I remade Taylor’s Computer Science website from the ground up and maintained it. I also assisted in building the front end of a testing website for COS students. I am currently helping LifeChange Counsel rebuild their website.

Post Production

I have significant experience in film, photography, and audio post production and editing. I have used Avid Media Composer and Pro Tools, Adobe Premiere Pro, and Photoshop, and Affinity Photo

Teacher

Graphic Design and Computer Graphics

I made marketing materials for Taylor’s Computer Science Department, and frequently do small jobs for others as needed. I have significant experience in tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, as well as Blender.

Media Services

I serviced Campus Events, setting up, maintaining, and tearing down sound, projection, and other media equipment. I have often run sound and projection in other areas as needed.
